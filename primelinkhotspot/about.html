About Prime Link Hotspot

Prime Link Hotspot is a locally-owned internet service providing fast, reliable, and affordable WiFi to Kayanja residents. We empower students, professionals, and families to stay connected easily.

Our mission is to make internet access simple and affordable, while maintaining a stable connection for all users.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Provide affordable, reliable, and high-speed internet to everyone in Kayanja.

Vision

Become the leading hotspot service in Kayanja, keeping the community connected anytime, anywhere.
